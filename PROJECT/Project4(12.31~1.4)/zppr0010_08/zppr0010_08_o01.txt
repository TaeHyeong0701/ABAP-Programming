module MODIFY_SCREEN output.
LOOP AT SCREEN.
************************************************************************
* Hide Screen Layout : p_mode (input-field), Text1(text-field)
************************************************************************
  if SCREEN-NAME = 'P_MODE' OR screen-name = 'TEXT1'.
  IF radio2 = 'X'.
************************************************************************

  ENDIF.
  ENDIF.
